Usage notes

A quadrature encoder uses a wheel with teeth to indicate the position of the shaft (high and low side, or black and white in the figure below). This wheel is emulated by the QAE FPGA code module. The QAD module will measure channels A and B and generate signals 'x1', 'x2', or 'x4', depending on configuration.

 

 

The QAD signals are generated as follows:

•x1: a pulse for each rising edge on channel A

•x2: a pulse for each rising and falling edge on channel A

•x4: a pulse for each rising and falling edge on both A and B channels
